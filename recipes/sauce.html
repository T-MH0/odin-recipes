Sauce Recipe

How to make fried chicken sauce at home

By T-MH0

Here is a quick and easy sauce to finish up this fried chicken meal. Paired with a good sauce and fries, your dinner will blow your family away. They will never question your cooking abilities again.

Here's the overview

  1. Ingredients
  2. Making the sauce

See? This will be easy!

What you will need

  1. 1/2 cup of mayo
  2. 1/4 cup ketchup
  3. 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  4. 1/4 teaspoon worcesterchire sauce
  5. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Here's how you'll do it

  • Step one is to put all the ingredients together in a bowl and blend until one color
  • Step two is to serve with your food

Really, that's all there is to it!
